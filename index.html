Well Come To Our Website

Welcome to the website of CeylonShipping Corporation Ltd. Ceylon Shipping Corporation Ltd (CSCL) is the oldest national carrier of Sri Lanka. The inauguration of Ceylon Shipping Corporation (CSC) as a fully state-owned statutory corporation took place on 6th June 1971, in accordance with Ceylon Shipping Corporation Act, No.11 of 1971. The government of Sri Lanka holds all stakes.
